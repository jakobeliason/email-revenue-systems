§ 07 · FAQ

Questions most people ask.

How fast will I see results?

Month 1 is designed to put cash on the board fast. We start with The Customer Comeback System™ — a quick-win campaign to customers who already know you and just need a reason to come back. Most clients see real revenue within the first 30 days.

What if I'm already emailing my list?

Most of our clients are — they're just doing it inconsistently. We don't replace what's working; we audit it, fix what isn't, and build the systems on top so the whole thing runs as one engine. If something is pulling its weight already, we keep it and amplify it.

Do I have to switch email platforms?

Probably not. We work with most major email and CRM platforms — Mailchimp, Klaviyo, ActiveCampaign, HubSpot, Constant Contact, and more. If we recommend a switch, we'll explain exactly why and handle the migration ourselves.

Who writes the emails — you or me?

We do. Strategy, copywriting, design, scheduling, sending — all included. You review and approve. The whole point is that the system runs without adding to your plate.

Is $497/mo really the all-in price?

Yes. Same monthly price during the 6-month build and during ongoing scaling. No upcharge for management, no premium tiers, no surprise add-ons. One price, both phases, every month.

How does the guarantee work?

If we haven't generated more revenue than the system has cost you over the first 6 months, we keep working for free until we do. That's the deal — and we put it in writing.

Can I cancel?

The build phase is a 6-month minimum because the system needs time to mature before it can be measured fairly. After month six, you're month-to-month. Cancel any time once the system is live — but most clients don't, because the system is generating more than it costs.

Who owns the system if we stop working together?

You do. Every system, every email, every automation lives in your account, on your platform. If we ever part ways, the entire system is yours to keep running.
